Research Interest

  • Utilizing advanced TCAD tools and atomistic simulation software (e.g., Sentaurus TCAD, QuantumATK) to investigate the quantum transport behavior and device physics of silicon-based nano-devices, including FinFETs and Gate-All-Around (GAA) architectures.
  • Developing in-house quantum transport simulation programs and numerical solvers based on the k·p-NEGF framework. (Coupled mode space method, Poisson solvers: Gummel type).
  • Investigating quantum transport mechanisms about spintronics devices, with a particular focus on conventional magnetic tunnel junctions (MTJs), antiferromagnetic tunnel junctions, and emerging altermagnetic tunnel junctions.
